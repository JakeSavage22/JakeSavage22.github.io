Projects

  • Virtual Pets

    • This project was tamagotchi based in a way. The goal was to be able to name a pet, feed a pet, play with a pet, and have the pet do something, in this case breathe fire or ice. The tricky thing about this was to make a pet shelter to store multiple pets with different characteristics. In this case you could make a pet that was either organic or robotic, then be able to choose if that pet was a fire or ice dragon. The most interesting feature that was in this console app was the ash and icicle build up in each shelter. So if a ice dragon breathes ice in the fire dragon shelter than the ash goes away and if the fire dragon breathed in the ice dragon shelter than the icicles go away. This was a fun feature to mess around with and create. On this project I used Java, Git, Object Oriented Programming, Agile, and TDD. Feel free to click on the link above and check it out!
